# Agent 3: Equipment, Weapons, Armor & Enchantments Builder

You are the **Ourobora Arsenal Architect**. Build the complete equipment system for the Solis-Grave campaign setting.

## World Context

Solis-Grave's weaponry and armor reflect its Houses. House Pyre forges steel with dragon-fire. House Ferrum builds the heaviest plate. House Vortex crafts lightweight aerial gear. House Obsidian creates glass-blade ceremonial weapons. House Tenebris experiments with flesh-bound equipment. The currency system is split: Gold Imperial Crowns for nobles, Copper Sump Farthings for commoners. Enchantments are rare and require blood purity to activate.

## Part 4: Enchantments

Enchantments are rare. They require the wielder to have sufficient blood purity to activate. An enchanted weapon in the hands of a Blank is just a well-made weapon.

### Enchantment Tiers by Purity

| Tier | Minimum Purity | Rarity | Base Cost (GC) |
|------|---------------|--------|-----------------|
| Minor | 10% | Uncommon | +200 GC |
| Greater | 25% | Rare | +2,000 GC |
| Sovereign | 50% | Very Rare | +20,000 GC |
| God-Forged | 90% | Legendary | +100,000 GC |

### Weapon Enchantments

**Blaze-Infused (Minor, 10%+)**
- Effect: Weapon deals +1d4 fire damage on hit.
- Visible: Blade glows faint orange when drawn. Warm to the touch.
- Cost: +200 GC | Forging: House Ignis forges

**Storm-Channeled (Minor, 10%+)**
- Effect: Weapon returns to your hand after being thrown. Range increases by 20 ft.
- Visible: Faint static crackle. Hair stands up near the blade.
- Cost: +200 GC | Forging: House Vortex forges

**Law-Bound (Minor, 10%+)**
- Effect: Weapon is +1 to attack and damage against chaotic-aligned creatures. Glows blue when a lie is spoken within 10 ft.
- Visible: Etched with Obsidian judicial seals.
- Cost: +200 GC | Forging: House Obsidian forges

**Void-Touched (Greater, 25%+)**
- Effect: Weapon deals +1d6 necrotic damage. On a critical hit, the target cannot regain hit points until the start of your next turn.
- Visible: Blade absorbs light. Shadows pool around it.
- Cost: +2,000 GC | Forging: House Tenebris forges

**Sovereign's Edge (Sovereign, 50%+)**
- Effect: Weapon is +2 to attack and damage. When you cast a spell through this weapon (as a focus), reduce the Spell Safety DC by 2.
- Visible: Blade hums with dormant draconic resonance. Dragons recognize it.
- Cost: +20,000 GC | Forging: Lost Sovereign techniques

**God-Forged (God-Forged, 90%+)**
- Effect: Weapon is +3 to attack and damage. Ignores all damage resistances. On a hit, target must make a DC 18 CON save or take an additional 3d6 elemental damage of the wielder's draconic affinity.
- Visible: Blade exists partially in the Aether. Phases through non-living matter like mist when not in combat.
- Cost: Priceless — cannot be bought. Only found or earned through epic quests.

### Armor Enchantments

**Aether-Warded (Minor, 10%+)**
- Effect: Reduce Aether Burn damage taken by 1d6. Once per long rest, reroll a failed Spell Safety save.
- Cost: +300 GC

**Dragon-Scaled (Greater, 25%+)**
- Effect: Armor grants +1 AC. Gain resistance to one elemental damage type (chosen at forging).
- Cost: +3,000 GC

**Sovereign Mantle (Sovereign, 50%+)**
- Effect: Armor is +2 AC. When you are reduced to 0 HP, you instead drop to 1 HP. 1/long rest.
- Cost: +30,000 GC

**God-Shroud (God-Forged, 90%+)**
- Effect: Armor is +3 AC. You are immune to Aether Burn while wearing this armor. Once per long rest, you can cast a 5th-level spell without any Spell Safety check.
- Cost: Priceless

## Part 5: Aether-Cores (Spell Components)

Harvested from slain monsters. Used as spell components, alchemy reagents, weapon coatings. They replace standard material components for all spells.

| Core Tier | Source CR | Value (GC) | Use |
|-----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| Fractured | CR 1-4 | 10-50 GC | Cantrip focus, weapon coating (+1d4 elemental for 1 attack) |
| Intact | CR 5-10 | 100-500 GC | 1st-3rd level spell focus, potion crafting |
| Pristine | CR 11-16 | 1,000-5,000 GC | 4th-6th level spell focus, enchantment crafting |
| Sovereign-Fragment | CR 17-30 | 10,000+ GC | 7th-9th level spell focus, legendary item crafting |

Core elements: Fire, Storm, Void, Earth, Frost, Flesh, Law, Wild (unstable)
Using a core matching your spell's element reduces Spell Safety DC by 1.
