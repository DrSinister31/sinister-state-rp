Berts Vehicle Rentals is open for business! Following the failure of Escalera Rent-A-Car's hydrogen fuel fleet rollout, all locations across San Andreas were purchased by Berts to bring gas guzzlin' diesel vehicles back!

Video Preview: https://youtu.be/1TqnTbAay_U

# Features:
- Interior and parking lot modified to Berts branding
- New Mule included, with Berts branding (useful for vehicle/truck rental scripts) (spawn code: bertsmule)
- Rental Bus retexture (in the berts_car_rental Stream file) 
- New interior for rental office
