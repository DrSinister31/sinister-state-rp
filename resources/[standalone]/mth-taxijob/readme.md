# Taxi Job

A simple Taxi Job for FiveM.

## 📌 Features

- Fully Open-Source
- Easy Configuration
- CS & EN Locales
- Configurable Customer models
- Configurable Payment amount (No Payment as well)
- Configurable Vehicle model
- Configurable Pickup & Dropoff locations

## 📦 Resource Information

- **Name:** Taxi Job  
- **Author:** myth1caldev  
- **Version:** 1.0.0  
- **Game:** FiveM  
- **Framework:** ESX / QBcore  
- **Dependencies:** ox_lib, ox_target, ox_inventory 

## 🔧 How It Works

1. Start working as a Taxi driver
2. Get inside your Taxi vehicle
3. Pick a customer up
4. Drive him to his destination
5. Get paid
