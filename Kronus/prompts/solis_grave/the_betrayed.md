# Nyx, The Betrayed — DM Mechanics Reference

## Identity

| Trait | Value |
|-------|-------|
| True Name | Nyx |
| Titles | The Betrayed, The Sleeper, The Tail, The Sixth |
| Domain | Void, Time, Cycles (the ouroboros) |
| Element | Void — registers as "nothing" on standard tests |
| Symbol | Serpent eating its tail |
| Ally | Viridomir, the Wild-King (Nature God, deceased) |
| Status | Essence scattered across mortal bloodlines. Slowly recombining in a vessel. |
| Church Position | NEVER EXISTED. Heresy. |
| Cult Position | The murdered god. Waiting to rise. |

## The Vessel (Elected Player)

### What The Player Knows
Nothing. They created a character with normal purity (whatever they rolled). They believe they are a regular Blank or Common.

### What The DM Knows

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| `is_void_marked: true` | Hidden on Character Sheet |
| Public `blood_purity` | Whatever the player rolled (displayed normally) |
| True nature | Void-coded — invisible to Blood Crystals |
| `is_sovereign: false` | NOT a Sovereign. This is different. |
| `dual_heartbeat_active: true` | Void resonance pulse, not draconic heartbeat |

### Void Mechanics (All Behind Screen)

| Trigger | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Blood Crystal test | Reads as 0-12% (public purity). Crystal flickers — Church NPCs sense "wrongness" but can't articulate it. A Void-specific crystal (Cult artifact, nearly impossible to find) would read true. |
| Beasts within 30 ft. | Auto-submit. Viridomir's children remember Nyx. The wolf lowers its head. The warhorse goes quiet. The drake flinches. |
| Abominations within 500 ft. | Aggro-lock. The Void essence is the "cleanest" purity to their corrupted palates. They will bulldoze through anything to reach the vessel. |
| Dragon encounters | Sovereigns: recognize the Void and fall silent. May bow. Scions: fascinated/horrified. Archons: blind to it. Wyverns/Drakes: frightened (WIS save or cower). |
| Church NPCs (Inquisitors) | Unsettled. Make Insight checks. On 15+, they "feel something is deeply wrong" but can't identify it. On natural 20, they taste copper and hear a distant heartbeat. |
| Spell casting | Vessel CAN cast if their public purity permits. Spell Safety DC uses PUBLIC purity. Spells sometimes manifest with Void "spill" — nearby candles dim, shadows stretch toward the caster. |
| Dreams | Random during long rests. The DM may deliver fragments: fire and screaming, a serpent eating its tail, five figures standing over someone, a voice whispering "remember." |
| "Dig Deep" chant | If overheard, vessel makes WIS save DC 10. On success: chills, blurred vision, flash of a face they don't recognize (Nyx's). On failure: no reaction. |

## Signs The DM Can Drop (Escalating)

### Early Signs (Session 1-5)
- A stray dog follows the character and won't leave.
- An Inquisitor stares too long during a routine patrol.
- A candle goes out when the character enters a room.
- "Dig Deep" chant overheard — the character feels cold.

### Mid Signs (Session 6-15)
- A Wyvern breaks off an attack to stare at the character, then flies away.
- A Church Blood-Judge demands a "random purity test" on the party — specifically interested in this character.
- The character dreams of a serpent eating its tail. Wakes with their hand over their own heart.
- A Cult recruiter approaches them in secret. "You carry something. You don't know what. But WE do."

### Late Signs (Session 16+)
- A Sovereign Dragon (if encountered) speaks directly to the character: "You wear his face wrong, little one. But I see him underneath."
- An Abomination engages — and screams Nyx's name while attacking.
- The character's own Blood Crystal shatters when they touch it.
- The heartbeat becomes audible to others. Party members hear it during quiet moments.

## The Awakening

If/when the player discovers their nature:

| Before (Dormant) | After (Awakened) |
|---|---|
| Public purity displayed | True Void nature revealed |
| No special abilities | Void domain spells (DM's curated list) |
| Beasts submit passively | Can command beasts (Viridomir connection) |
| Dragons sense wrongness | Dragons know EXACTLY who they are |
| Church NPCs unsettled | Church mobilizes within 24 hours |
| Cult watches from shadows | Cult makes open contact |
| Abominations aggro | Abominations aggro HARDER (Void is now "loud") |
| Dual-Heartbeat random | Can suppress or amplify at will |
| Dreams are vague | Dreams become direct communication with Nyx's fragmented consciousness |

## DM Reminders

- The elected player must NEVER be told. Signs emerge through narrative only.
- This is NOT a Sovereign. No Dormancy, no Surge, no -4 penalty. Void is its own thing.
- Cult membership by other players is separate from the vessel. A Cult member could be worshipping Nyx without knowing the vessel is in their party.
- The Church is the primary threat. They've been erasing Nyx for 500 years. One slip and the Inquisitors descend.
- The prophecy is real and gradually fulfilling. Let it play out over the campaign's full arc — years, not sessions.
