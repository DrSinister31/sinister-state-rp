## 🌑 The World

The dying sun of Ourobora casts long shadows over the continent of Solis-Grave. Here, magic is not learned — it is **born**. Dragon blood runs through every human vein, and your percentage of purity, revealed on your fifteenth birthday — the **Day of Ascension** — determines your caste, your rights, and whether you will ever wield a spark of the ancient fire.

The **Church of the Five Skulls** teaches that five Sovereign Dragons willingly sacrificed their physical forms to anchor reality itself. Their essence scattered through mortal bloodlines, gifting humanity with magic. The Church maintains order through **Inquisitors** — holy executioners who hunt illegal spellcasters and enforce the purity caste system with absolute authority.

But the continent is not at peace. **House Ignis**, the ruling fire dynasty, conducts scorched-earth purges in the border valleys, hunting for hidden bloodlines. The **Cult of the Sixth**, an outlawed underground movement, whispers prophecies of a betrayed god who will return to unmake everything the Church built. And you — whether a 0% Blank or a high-blood Archon — are caught in the grinding gears of a world that does not care if you live or die.

Your journey begins at the **Citadel of the Dragon-Garrison**, a brutalist military academy carved into a frozen cliffside. {{starting_location_context}}

## 🏛️ The Six Great Houses

All six houses publicly bow to the Flame Throne of House Ignis. Privately, they scheme, spy, and sabotage each other with centuries of accumulated hatred.

### House Ignis — The Iron-Blaze
**Domain:** Scorched-earth military conquest. Fire magic. The ruling dynasty.
**Motto:** *"Ash and Submission."*
**Hates:** Rival houses with rising power. Hidden bloodlines. Anyone who challenges their throne.
**Trait — Blaze Smite:** Once per long rest, add 1d4 fire damage to a successful martial strike.

### House Vortex — The Storm-Crest
**Domain:** High-altitude sky networks, logistical coastlines, airborne fleets.
**Motto:** *"We Rule the Wind, We Own the Horizon."*
**Hates:** Ignis, for controlling the sky routes they pioneered. Constantly scheming.
**Trait — Rider's Instinct:** Gain Animal Handling proficiency; mounted speed increases by 10 feet.

### House Obsidian — The Eclipse Nobles
**Domain:** Imperial law, judicial courts, blood purity arrays.
**Motto:** *"Law Bends to the Pure."*
**Hates:** Ignis, for overruling their judicial authority when politically convenient.
**Trait — Law-Bender:** Permanent +2 bonus to all Deception and Insight checks.

### House Tenebris — The Abyssal Deep
**Domain:** Clandestine flesh-fusion mutation labs in the isolated northern wastes.
**Motto:** *"Truth Bleeds in the Dark."*
**Hates:** **Everyone.** Banished north for their experiments. Seething with cold rage.
**Trait — Flesh Anchor:** Advantage on saving throws against poison and elemental acid.

### House Ferrum — The Iron-Bound
**Domain:** Frontline shield-wall defense guarding the desolate monster borders.
**Motto:** *"Iron Endures the Storm."*
**Hates:** Ignis, for abandoning the frontier. Their soldiers die while Ignis nobles feast.
**Trait — Ferrum Defiance:** Once per long rest, drop to 1 HP instead of 0 HP when struck.

### House Pyre — The Foundry Lords
**Domain:** Industrial blast furnaces, dragon chain forges, arms manufacturing.
**Motto:** *"Forged in Blood, Calloused in Flame."*
**Hates:** No one. They sell weapons to everyone and pretend neutrality.
**Trait — Foundry Hardened:** Permanent Fire resistance; react to deal 1d4 fire damage to close attackers.

## 📜 The Caste System

Your blood purity percentage determines your legal rights, social standing, and access to magic.

| Caste | Purity | Rights |
|---|---|---|
| **Blank** | 0% | Legally non-persons. Property, not people. Cannot cast magic. Branded with owner's sigil. |
| **Common** | 1-14% | Full legal rights. Cannot legally cast magic. Peasants, artisans, merchants. |
| **Lesser Blood** | 15-39% | May cast cantrips. 1st+ spells require Church permit. Minor houses, low-tier officers. |
| **Archon** | 40-85% | Full magic access. Noble status. May own Blanks. May serve in government. |
| **Scion** | 90-94% | Immediate Church custody. Classified. Cataclysmic power, unstable biology. |
| **Sovereign** | 95-100% | Functionally extinct per Church doctrine. A living god in human skin. **Hunted.** |
