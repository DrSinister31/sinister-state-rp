### Crystal-Infused Hydra
*Huge monstrosity, unaligned*  
**AC** 10 | **HP** ? | **Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.  
**STR** ? | **DEX** ? | **CON** ? | **INT** ? | **WIS** ? | **CHA** ?  
**CR** 0 (10000 XP)

**Traits:**  
- *Crystalline Growth.* At the start of each of its turns, the hydra grows one new head for each head lost since its last turn (max 10 heads). Each head has 25 hit points. While the hydra has more than one head, it has advantage on saving throws against being blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, stunned, and knocked unconscious.
- *Crystal Shard Regeneration.* If the hydra takes cold damage, it regrows one head at the start of its next turn. If it takes radiant damage, it loses one head (if it has any) and its maximum hit points are reduced by 5 per head lost until it finishes a long rest.
- *Multiheaded.* The hydra can make as many bite attacks per round as it has heads. It can also use its Crystal Spray action as many times as it has heads, but each head can only use it once per turn.
- *Reflective Scales.* Any time a creature targets the hydra with a spell that requires a ranged spell attack, roll a d6. On a 1-2, the spell is reflected back at the caster as if the caster were the target.

**Actions:**  
- *Multiattack.* The hydra makes as many bite attacks as it has heads, and can use Crystal Spray up to that many times.
- *Bite.* Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8 + 6) piercing damage plus 5 (1d10) radiant damage.
- *Crystal Spray.* The hydra spews a spray of razor-sharp crystal shards from one of its heads in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) slashing damage and 10 (3d6) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Hit: 22 (5d8) necrotic damage and the target must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution saving throw or have its hit point maximum reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken.
