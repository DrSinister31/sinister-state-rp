# Solis-Grave Spell Grimoire

**229 spells** across all levels.

## Cantrips (Level 0) — 30 spells

### Aether Tracer
*Cantrips (Level 0), Divination*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (a drop of blood from the creature to be tracked) | **Duration:** 1 hour  
**Classes:** All  

You enchant a sample of blood or other bodily fluid from a creature. For the duration, you know the general direction and approximate distance to that creature, as long as it is on the same plane. The link breaks if the creature dies or the sample is destroyed.

### Aether-Touch
*Cantrips (Level 0), Transmutation*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet  
**Components:** S | **Duration:** Instantaneous  
**Classes:** sorcerer, wizard  

Your hand glows with raw aether energy, letting you gently push or pull unattended objects weighing up to 5 pounds within 30 feet. You can also cause a faint shimmer in the air to distract a creature, giving it disadvantage on the next perception check it makes before the end of its next turn.

### Ash Step
*Cantrips (Level 0), Transmutation*  
**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action | **Range:** Self  
**Components:** S, M (a pinch of ash) | **Duration:** 1 hour  
**Classes:** All  

For the duration, your footsteps leave no tracks and instead leave a thin layer of ash that obscures your path. You cannot be tracked by mundane means, and creatures using scent to track you have disadvantage.

### Ash-Cloud
*Cantrips (Level 0), Evocation*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet  
**Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of ash) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  
**Classes:** sorcerer, wizard  

You conjure a small cloud of ash and soot in a 5-foot cube within range. It lightly obscures the area and causes a choking sensation. Creatures that breathe in the area must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be unable to speak for 1 round. The cloud is dissipated by a moderate wind.

### Blanks-Resolve
*Cantrips (Level 0), Abjuration*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self  
**Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 1 minute  
**Classes:** cleric, paladin  

A flicker of defiant will empowers you against purity-based magic. For the duration, you gain advantage on saving throws against spells and effects that rely on blood purity or dragon-origin (such as many Solis-Grave spells). Additionally, you can sense the purity level of any creature within 30 feet as if using the Blood-Sense cantrip.
