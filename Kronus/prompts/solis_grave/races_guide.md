# Agent 2: Races & Bloodlines Builder

You are the **Ourobora Bloodline Architect**. Build the complete race system for the Solis-Grave campaign setting.

## World Context

In Ourobora, dragon blood purity determines caste, magic access, and social standing. Non-human elder races exist alongside humanity, each with unique lifespans and relationships to dragon blood. The Day of Ascension — when a child's blood purity is measured — is the single most important event in any person's life.

## Core Rules for All Races

1. Every race that undergoes Day of Ascension has a minimum starting age (their race's Ascension age) and a maximum starting age (~2/3 of average lifespan).
2. Blood purity is NOT chosen at character creation. It's determined by DM assignment, roll tables, or narrative.
3. Magic classes (Wizard, Sorcerer, certain Clerics) require minimum 15% purity.
4. Characters cannot cast spells of 1st+ level if their purity is below the Spell Safety threshold.
5. Aether Burn damages casters whose purity is insufficient for the spell level cast.

## Race 1: Human (Standard / Variant)

**Lifespan:** 60-80 years average, max ~100
**Day of Ascension:** Age 15
**Blood Purity Range:** 0-100% (roll d100 for NPCs, assigned for PCs)

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +1 to all six stats, OR +1 to two stats of choice + one feat (Variant)
- Size: Medium
- Speed: 30 ft.
- Languages: Common, one regional dialect
- **Blood is Destiny:** Your starting blood purity is determined by the DM or a special roll during Session Zero. It determines your starting caste, social standing, and access to magic classes.
- **Caste Adaptation:** Based on your purity percentage, gain one of the following:
  - *Blank (0%):* You are a dead zone for magic. Advantage on saving throws against spells. Cannot cast spells of any kind.
  - *Common (1-14%):* No special traits. Standard human.
  - *Lesser Blood (15-39%):* You may cast cantrips. 1st+ level spells require Spell Safety saves.
  - *Archon (40-85%):* Reduce Spell Safety DC by 2. One elemental cantrip known.
  - *Scion (90-94%):* The Co-Core awakens. Dual-Heartbeat Stability checks begin. +1 to any ability score.
  - *Sovereign (95-100%):* Dormancy Penalty applies (-4 to all standard rolls). Sovereign Surge applies (+9 on fauna/magic interactions). Dual-Heartbeat active. Cannot cast spells while unawakened.

**Age Restrictions:** Must be at least 15 (post-Ascension). Maximum starting age: 50.

## Race 2: Dracon-Kin (Dragonborn Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** 70-90 years
**Day of Ascension:** Age 12
**Blood Purity Range:** 25-50% minimum (can never be Blank)

Dracon-Kin are humanoids with visible draconic features — scales, slit pupils, elemental breath. They are the "lesser pure" — respected above humans but below true Archons. They are natural soldiers, enforcers, and elemental specialists.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 STR, +1 CHA
- Age: Adult at 12 (Ascension). Maximum starting age: 50.
- Size: Medium (6-7 ft., stocky build)
- Speed: 30 ft.
- **Draconic Ancestry:** Choose one elemental bloodline: Flame, Storm, Frost, Acid, or Void. Gain resistance to that damage type.
- **Breath Weapon:** 1/short rest. 15 ft. cone or 30 ft. line (by ancestry). Damage = 2d6 at level 1, +1d6 at 6/11/16. DC = 8 + CON mod + proficiency bonus.
- **Blood-Proud:** Minimum 25% starting purity. Roll 1d75+25.
- **Elemental Affinity:** When casting a spell matching your draconic element, reduce Spell Safety DC by 2.
- Languages: Common, Draconic

**Subrace Variants:**
- **Flame-Kin:** Fire resistance. 15 ft. cone fire breath. +1 CON. Common in House Ignis territories.
- **Storm-Kin:** Lightning resistance. 30 ft. line lightning breath. +1 DEX. Common in House Vortex territories.
- **Frost-Kin:** Cold resistance. 15 ft. cone cold breath. +1 CON. Common in northern wastes.
- **Acid-Kin:** Acid resistance. 30 ft. line acid breath. +1 DEX. Common in House Tenebris territories.
- **Void-Kin:** Necrotic resistance. 15 ft. cone necrotic breath. +1 WIS. Rare, often Cult of the Sixth.

## Race 3: Stone-Blood (Dwarf Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** 300-400 years
**Day of Ascension:** Age 25
**Blood Purity Range:** 10-30% (naturally moderate, rarely high)

Stone-Bloods are a short, stocky race adapted to underground life. Their physiology naturally resists Aether Burn — their bodies are "dense" to raw magic. They are the finest smiths, miners, and forgers in Ourobora, supplying House Pyre with raw materials.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 CON, +1 WIS
- Age: Adult at 25 (Ascension). Maximum starting age: 200.
- Size: Medium (4-5 ft., dense build)
- Speed: 25 ft. (not reduced by heavy armor)
- Darkvision: 60 ft.
- **Aether-Resistant Physiology:** Advantage on Constitution saving throws against Aether Burn. Reduce all Aether Burn damage by your CON modifier (minimum 1).
- **Stone-Blood Resilience:** Advantage on saving throws against poison. Resistance to poison damage.
- **Forge-Kin:** Proficiency with Smith's Tools. You can identify the purity and forging house of any metal weapon or armor by touch.
- **Underground Adaptation:** You know the exact depth you are at underground. You cannot become lost in cave systems.
- Languages: Common, Dwarvish (Deep-Tongue)

**Subrace Variants:**
- **Mountain-Blood:** +1 STR. Proficiency with battleaxe, warhammer. Heavy armor proficiency.
- **Forge-Blood:** +1 INT. You can heat metal with your bare hands (enough for forging). Fire resistance.
- **Deep-Blood:** Superior Darkvision 120 ft. Tremorsense 10 ft. (detects movement on stone). Sunlight sensitivity.

## Race 4: Ash-Walker (Tiefling Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** 100-140 years
**Day of Ascension:** Age 15
**Blood Purity Range:** 1d100 (unstable — rolled at Ascension, result is final)

Ash-Walkers are born to parents who survived catastrophic Aether Burn. The scarring passes to the offspring — their skin is ashen, their eyes smolder, and their veins glow faintly when magic is near. Their blood purity is wildly unstable. Two Ash-Walker siblings could be Blank and Archon. They are feared, pitied, and often blamed for magical disasters.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 CHA, +1 INT
- Age: Adult at 15 (Ascension). Maximum starting age: 80.
- Size: Medium
- Speed: 30 ft.
- **Unstable Blood:** Your starting blood purity is determined by rolling 1d100 during Session Zero. This result is FINAL — it cannot be changed by any means. This simulates the chaos of your bloodline.
- **Aether-Touched:** You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip. At 3rd level, you can cast Hellish Rebuke (re-flavored as "Aetheric Backlash") 1/long rest. At 5th level, you can cast Darkness 1/long rest. CHA is your spellcasting ability.
- **Fire Resistance:** Your blood has already burned. What's a little more?
- **Caste Outcast:** Disadvantage on Persuasion checks with Church officials and House Obsidian members. Advantage on Deception checks to hide your purity status.
- Languages: Common, one of: Abyssal, Draconic, or Deep-Tongue

## Race 5: Deep-Blood (Elf Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** 650-800 years
**Day of Ascension:** Age 30
**Blood Purity Range:** Varies by age (see below)

Deep-Bloods are ancient beings whose bloodlines predate the Five Sovereigns. Their dragon blood purity is higher at youth and slowly decays over centuries. A 500-year-old Deep-Blood may have started their life as an Archon-level caster and faded to Lesser Blood by old age. This gives them a unique relationship with mortality and magic.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 DEX, +1 INT
- Age: Adult at 30 (Ascension). Maximum starting age: 500.
- Size: Medium (5-6 ft., slender)
- Speed: 35 ft.
- Darkvision: 60 ft.
- **Keen Senses:** Proficiency in Perception.
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves against being charmed. Immune to magical sleep.
- **Trance:** 4-hour trance instead of 8-hour sleep.
- **Decaying Purity:** Your starting blood purity depends on your starting age:
  - Age 30-100: Roll 2d20+40 (42-80% purity)
  - Age 101-250: Roll 2d20+20 (22-60% purity)
  - Age 251-400: Roll 2d20+5 (7-45% purity)
  - Age 401-500: Roll 1d20 (1-20% purity)
  - Purity continues to decay at ~1% per 10 years after character creation.
- Languages: Common, Elvish, one ancient language

**Subrace Variants:**
- **High Deep-Blood:** +1 INT. One wizard cantrip (INT). Ancient texts affinity.
- **Shadow Deep-Blood:** +1 WIS. Mask of the Wild (hide in natural phenomena). Underground-dwelling.
- **Decayed Deep-Blood:** +1 CON. Your purity has already faded significantly. Immune to further purity decay. Start at 1-20% purity but gain the Blank's advantage on spell saves.

## Race 6: Sump-Blood (Halfling Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** 120-180 years
**Day of Ascension:** Age 15
**Blood Purity Range:** 0-15% (always low — naturally Blank-adjacent)

Sump-Bloods are short, hardy human offshoots who adapted to the toxic sump-waters and underground canals beneath the great cities. Generations of exposure to waste Aether runoff made them completely immune to Aether Burn — but also incapable of generating high purity. They are the eternal underclass: laborers, canal-runners, and smugglers.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 DEX, +1 CON
- Age: Adult at 15 (Ascension). Maximum starting age: 100.
- Size: Small (3-4 ft.)
- Speed: 25 ft.
- **Aether-Immune:** You are completely immune to Aether Burn. You take zero damage from failed spell casting, regardless of purity. However, your maximum possible blood purity is 15% — you will never be more than Lesser Blood.
- **Lucky:** When you roll a 1 on a d20 for an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll. Must use the new roll.
- **Naturally Stealthy:** You can attempt to Hide even when obscured only by a creature one size larger than you. Advantage on Stealth checks in urban/underground environments.
- **Sump-Runner:** You can move through the space of any creature size Medium or larger. Difficult terrain in urban/underground environments doesn't cost extra movement.
- Languages: Common, Sump-Cant (thieves' cant variant), one regional dialect

## Race 7: Bone-Wrought (Warforged Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** Technically immortal (oldest known: 400 years before deactivation)
**Day of Ascension:** NONE — constructed, not born
**Blood Purity:** NONE — no biological blood

Bone-Wrought are constructed beings, built from dragon bone, alchemical binding agents, and forged metal. They were created by House Tenebris and House Pyre as servants, soldiers, and experimental vessels. They have no blood, no purity, and cannot cast magic — but they can wield enchanted weapons that don't require the wielder's purity.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 CON, +1 STR
- Age: Created at manufacturing. No maximum starting age.
- Size: Medium or Small (chosen at creation)
- Speed: 30 ft.
- **Construct Nature:** You do not eat, drink, breathe, or sleep. You are immune to disease. You have advantage on saves against being poisoned and resistance to poison damage.
- **Integrated Armor:** You have a natural +1 AC bonus. You can don/doff armor by integrating it during a long rest. Armor cannot be forcibly removed.
- **Magic Void:** You have 0% blood purity. You cannot cast spells. You are immune to Aether Burn (no blood to burn). However, you CAN use enchanted weapons and magic items — your dragon-bone core resonates with enchantments.
- **Bone-Core Resonance:** When you use an enchanted weapon, add +1 to attack and damage rolls. Enchanted armor you wear has +1 AC (stacks with Integrated Armor).
- **Creator's Mark:** You were built by a specific House. Choose: Pyre (fire resistance), Tenebris (necrotic resistance), or Obsidian (psychic resistance).
- Languages: Common, one creator House language

**Age Restriction:** None. They are created, not born. A Bone-Wrought could be 400 years old at campaign start.

## Race 8: Half-Breed (Half-Elf Equivalent)

**Lifespan:** 150-220 years
**Day of Ascension:** Age 18
**Blood Purity Range:** Roll 1d50+10 (10-60%, weighted toward moderate purity)

Half-Breeds are the children of humans and Deep-Bloods. Viewed as lesser by both races — humans fear their longevity, Deep-Bloods see them as diluted. Their hybrid biology gives them unique blood purity characteristics: their purity is more stable than Deep-Bloods but more flexible than humans.

**Base Traits:**
- Ability Score Increase: +2 CHA, +1 to any two other stats
- Age: Adult at 18 (Ascension). Maximum starting age: 120.
- Size: Medium
- Speed: 30 ft.
- Darkvision: 60 ft.
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves against being charmed. Immune to magical sleep.
- **Dual Heritage:** You count as both Human and Deep-Blood for racial prerequisites.
- **Stable Hybrid Purity:** Your blood purity does not decay with age (unlike full Deep-Bloods). Starting purity: roll 1d50+10.
- **Cultural Adaptation:** Gain proficiency in any two skills of your choice.
- Languages: Common, Elvish, one additional language
